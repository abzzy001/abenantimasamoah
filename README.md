### 🚀 Featured Projects

🩺 **[Equitable AI for Pediatric Type 2 Diabetes Risk Prediction](https://github.com/abzzy001/Machine-Learning-Model-for-Pediatric-Type-2-Diabetes-Risk-Prediction)**
Fairness-aware ML model using multi-cycle NHANES data (2013–2018). Reached ~88% sensitivity with Logistic Regression & Random Forest; applied Fairlearn post-processing to reduce disparities across race, income, and sex subgroups.
`Python` `scikit-learn` `pandas` `Fairlearn` `Jupyter`

🛒 **[E-commerce Conversion Funnel & Cohort Retention Analysis](https://github.com/abzzy001/business_analytics_project)**
Analyzed 43,659 user events to evaluate conversion performance and retention. Surfaced the largest funnel drop-off (view → cart), exposed reliance on one-time buyers, and delivered 4 prioritized business recommendations.
`Excel` `Pivot Tables` `Cohort Analysis` `Funnel Analysis`

📊 **[Superstore Sales & Profitability Dashboard](https://github.com/abzzy001/Superstore_Tableau)**
Interactive Tableau dashboard combining KPI cards, time-series trends, and geographic heatmaps. Identified low-margin segments and regional profit gaps with self-service exploration via calculated fields and parameter controls.
`Tableau` `Data Storytelling` `EDA`

🏠 **[Airbnb Revenue & Neighborhood Insights](https://github.com/abzzy001/excel-airbnb-data-analysis)**
EDA on thousands of Airbnb listings using advanced Excel functions and pivot tables. Surfaced highest-margin segments across neighborhoods, property types, and price tiers to inform pricing strategy.
`Excel` `Pivot Tables` `EDA` `Data Cleaning`
